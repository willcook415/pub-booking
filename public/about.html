Welcome to The Curious Cat

Nestled between Herne Bay and Canterbury, The Curious Cat is a traditional country pub reborn with charm and character. Previously known as The First & Last, our pub was completely refurbished in 2019, blending countryside hospitality with a quirky modern twist.
