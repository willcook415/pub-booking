About Us

Welcome to The Curious Cat

Nestled in the heart of Whiskerbury, The Curious Cat is more than just a pub — it's a cosy retreat for lovers of great food, fine drink, and even better company. Established in 1892 and reborn in the modern age, we combine traditional English pub heritage with a tastefully contemporary twist.

Whether you’re popping in for a craft pint, savouring one of our award-winning roasts, or celebrating something special in our candlelit snug, you’ll feel at home here.

Our team is proud to champion local suppliers, hand-pull ales, and classic British fare made with seasonal ingredients. From Sunday jazz nights to our infamous pub quiz, there's always something going on — and a story to tell.

So come in, take a seat by the fireplace, and let us pour you a glass of something wonderful.
